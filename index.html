Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions patients ask about dental treatments at Aesthetica Dental Clinic, Punawale.

Is root canal treatment painful?

At Aesthetica Dental Clinic, root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is virtually painless. Most patients are surprised — the procedure is often more comfortable than a tooth extraction. Any mild soreness afterward resolves within 1–2 days.

How much does root canal treatment cost in Punawale?

Root canal treatment at Aesthetica costs between ₹3,500 and ₹8,000 depending on the tooth location and number of canals. Front teeth are simpler and cost less; molars with multiple canals cost more. We provide a full cost estimate during your consultation.

How many sittings does root canal treatment take?

Most root canals at Aesthetica are completed in 1–2 appointments, each lasting approximately 45–60 minutes, depending on the complexity and number of canals in the tooth.

How much do dental implants cost in Pune?

Dental implants at Aesthetica cost approximately ₹25,000 – ₹45,000 per tooth, including the titanium implant and crown. The exact cost depends on bone density, implant brand and crown type. We provide a detailed treatment plan before you commit.

Are clear aligners better than traditional braces?

Clear aligners are nearly invisible, removable and comfortable — ideal for mild-to-moderate alignment cases. Traditional braces are better for complex cases. Dr. Prachi will assess your teeth and recommend the best option at your consultation.

Is Aesthetica Dental Clinic open on Sundays?

Yes! Aesthetica is open every day of the week — Monday through Sunday, including public holidays — from 10:15 AM to 8:30 PM. Walk-ins are welcome; appointments are preferred.

Do you offer dental treatment for children?

Yes. We provide gentle paediatric dentistry for children of all ages in a calm, child-friendly environment. Early dental visits (from age 3–5) help prevent cavities and build healthy oral habits from the start.

How far is the clinic from Wakad and Hinjewadi?

Aesthetica Dental Clinic in Punawale is approximately 10–15 minutes from Wakad and 15–20 minutes from Hinjewadi IT Park (Phases 1, 2 & 3), making us the most convenient dental clinic for patients from these areas.
