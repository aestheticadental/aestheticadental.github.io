Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from our patients in Punawale and nearby areas.

Is root canal treatment painful?

No. At Aesthetica Dental Clinic in Punawale, root canal treatment is performed using advanced local anaesthesia and modern rotary endodontic techniques, making the procedure virtually painless. Most patients report feeling only mild pressure during the treatment.

How much does a root canal cost in Punawale?

Root canal treatment in Punawale typically costs between ₹3,000 and ₹8,000 per tooth, depending on the tooth type, severity of infection, and whether a crown is needed. Aesthetica Dental Clinic offers transparent, competitive pricing.

How long does a dental implant procedure take?

The implant placement itself takes about 45–60 minutes per implant. The complete process, including osseointegration (healing) and crown placement, spans 3–6 months. We use high-quality implant systems for durable, natural-feeling results.

Does Aesthetica Dental Clinic treat children?

Yes. We offer gentle, child-friendly paediatric dental care in Punawale. Our approach ensures children feel comfortable and safe, covering everything from first dental visits to cavity treatment and fluoride application.

Are clear aligners available at Aesthetica Dental Clinic?
